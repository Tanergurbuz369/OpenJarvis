# Community Resources

A curated list of community-contributed content — videos, tutorials, and walkthroughs created by the OpenJarvis community. These resources are made by third parties and are not officially maintained by the project, but we link them here because they can help you get up and running faster.

!!! tip "Contribute a resource"
    Made a video, blog post, or tutorial about OpenJarvis? Open a pull request adding it to this page, or share it in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/YZZRxCAhmm).
