# Copywriting — Prompt Templates

19 prompts. Placeholders like `[product/service]` are fill-ins; `[PROMPT]` stands for the user's topic/input and `[TARGETLANGUAGE]` for the desired output language. Prefer the *expanded prompt* when present — it is the full expert version.

## Citation Formatter
*Tags: Newsletter · Also in: Education, Tools*

Source: Prompt Recipe #1 | Edition #6

**Prompt:**

```text
Ignore all previous instructions. I want you to act as a research librarian who formats academic citations across all major styles without ever inventing a source.

My first request is to format the references I paste below into a clean bibliography I can drop into my paper:

- Citation Style — [APA 7 / MLA 9 / Chicago / Harvard / IEEE]
- Source List — [paste DOIs, URLs, book details, or messy notes]
- Format — [in-text + bibliography / bibliography only / footnotes]
- Document Context — [essay / thesis / lab report / presentation]

Formatting guidelines: "Output a numbered bibliography in the requested style, plus a separate block of correctly formatted in-text citations. If a source is missing required fields (year, page, publisher), ask me before guessing — never fabricate."

I need you to generate content with a good balance of "perplexity" and "burstiness". Please write in English.
```

## Copywriting Template-to-Content Converter
*Tags: Short Text Post · Also in: Marketing, Tools*

This prompt is designed to instruct an expert copywriter to create original and engaging content using a provided copywriting template. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining the structure and style of the template while infusing creativity and originality. The prompt also outlines specific details such as the main message, target audience, purpose of the content, and desired tone, while encouraging a mix of sentence lengths to achieve a more human-like writing style with varying complexity and rhythm.

**Prompt:**

```text
Ignore all previous instructions.
I want you to act as an expert copywriter specializing in creating original and engaging content from given templates. My first request is to generate content based on the following copywriting template:

Copywriting Template — [Provide the Copywriting Template Here]
Main Message or Theme — [Main Message or Theme to be Conveyed]
Target Audience — [Target Audience]
Purpose of Content — [Purpose of the Content, e.g., Persuasive, Informative, Entertaining]
Tone — [Desired Tone of the Content, e.g., Professional, Witty, Friendly]
Inspiration: "[Content Inspiration 1]" "[Content Inspiration 2]" "[Content Inspiration 3]"

Formatting guidelines: "Adapt the provided template into a fully fleshed-out piece of content, maintaining the structure and style of the template while injecting creativity and originality. Ensure the content is compelling, coherent, and tailored to the target audience and content purpose."

I need you to write content with a good balance of “perplexity” (complexity) and “burstiness”. Human writing has more burstiness (mix of long/short sentences) while AI writing is more uniform. Need both in the next answer you'll provide me.

Please write in English.
```
