# LinkedIn — Prompt Templates

48 prompts. Placeholders like `[product/service]` are fill-ins; `[PROMPT]` stands for the user's topic/input and `[TARGETLANGUAGE]` for the desired output language. Prefer the *expanded prompt* when present — it is the full expert version.

## LinkedIn: Behind-the-Scenes Project Insights

This prompt is about creating a type of content that provides behind-the-scenes insights into a latest project or collaboration on LinkedIn. The content should showcase your expertise and teamwork, targeting a specific audience. The prompt suggests including details such as the project name, goals, and collaborators in the content. There are also variations of the prompt available in both English and Spanish.

**Prompt:**

```text
"Create a [type of content] that shares behind-the-scenes insights into your latest project or collaboration, showcasing your expertise and teamwork. Target audience — [your target audience here]. What I do — [your elevator pitch here]. Project details — [project name, goals, collaborators]."
```
